Super Bugs

Science Lab

I attended an education excursion trip about Super Bugs. Superbugs are strains of bacteria, viruses, parasites and fungi that are resistant to most of the antibiotics and other medications commonly used to treat the infections they cause.

Carbapenem-resistant Enterobacteriaceae, ESBL-producing Enterobacteriaceae, Vancomycin-resistant Enterococcus and Methicillin-resistant Staphylococcus aureus are a few examples of super bugs. We learned about them and also participated in a quiz followed by a fun game of killing super bugs
